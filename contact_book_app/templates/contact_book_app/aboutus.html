About us

Contact management is a means of entering customer and lead data for storage, quick access, editing and tracking. Contact management can be as simple as a spreadsheet or as complex as customer relationship management (CRM) software. Contact management is the process of recording contacts' details and tracking their interactions with a business. The information can include addresses, phone numbers, emails, social media handles, orders, open quotes, sales history, associated companies, and so on.
